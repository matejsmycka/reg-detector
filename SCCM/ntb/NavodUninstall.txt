Odinstalace NTB z CS, spustit v PowerShell admin konzoli.
1. Odebrat z domény, provede se restart
	Remove-Computer -UnjoinDomaincredential %ucet_s_opravnenim_k_odebrani% -PassThru -Verbose -Restart -Force
2. Odinstalovat SCCM klienta
	C:\Windows\ccmsetup\ccmsetup.exe /uninstall
3. Odebrat certifikát
	Get-ChildItem cert:\localmachine\my | where-object {$_.subject -match 'staff.ucn.muni.cz'} | Remove-Item -Force
4. Odebrat VPN profil
	Get-VpnConnection -AllUserConnection | Where-Object {$_.serveraddress -match 'vpn.ucn.muni.cz'} | Remove-VpnConnection -Force

%ucet_s_opravnenim_k_odebrani% je napriklad staff\addtodomstaff
